HVAC and Plumbing business owners face three critical problems:

**Problem 1 — Missed Calls**
- Only 4% of people leave voicemails. The other 96% hang up and call the next number.
- Every missed call is a competitor's booking.
- The average HVAC or Plumbing owner misses 5–15 calls per week.
- Average job value: $850 per job.
- Cost of missing 5–15 calls/week: $22,000–$66,000 in lost revenue every month.

**Problem 2 — Owner Dependence**
- If the owner can't take a day off without the phones going dark, they don't own a business — they own a high-stress job with no sick days.
- Owner-dependence creates a hard growth ceiling.

**Problem 3 — No Systems**
- Manual follow-ups, sticky-note CRMs, and missed leads create zero visibility.
- Most owners guess at what's falling through the cracks. ProDispatcher measures it.

## SECTION 5: THE AI RECEPTIONIST — HOW IT WORKS

The ProDispatcher AI receptionist is a voice AI trained specifically for HVAC and Plumbing businesses.

**Core capabilities:**
- Answers every inbound call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year
- Greets callers professionally (e.g., "Thank you for calling! This is Sarah. How can I help you today?")
- Handles frequently asked questions about services, pricing, and availability
- Collects complete caller information: name, address, phone number, issue description
- Books appointments directly into the owner's calendar
- Logs every caller automatically into the CRM
- Filters spam calls

**Emergency handling:**
- Recognizes emergency keywords: "burst pipe", "no heat", "gas smell", "flooding", "no AC", "96°F"
- Responds with urgency and appropriate empathy
- Immediately notifies the owner via SMS and call with all collected details
- Lead captured and dispatched in under 90 seconds
- Estimated value saved per emergency capture: $1,700+
